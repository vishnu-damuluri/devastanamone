Sri Pallikondeshwara Swamy Devasthanam in Shurutupalli is esteemed as a sacred center of Vedic, Agamic, and mystical traditions. The deity, Sri Pallikondeshwara Swamy, is revered with profound devotion and spirituality. Devotees at this temple can receive the divine blessings of Sri Pallikondeshwara Swamy by participating in various rituals, including Rudraabhishekam, Rudra Homam, Kumkumarchana, Lakshabilva Archana, and other Pratyaksha Seva ceremonies, all conducted directly within the temple premises.
